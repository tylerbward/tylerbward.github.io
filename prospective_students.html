Prospective Students

I will be joining the Computer Science faculty in Morehead State University's Department of Engineering Sciences in Fall 2026, meaning I am actively looking for students to collaborate with on research. My research interests largely center on applications of artificial intelligence (AI) in the medical domain, with a particular emphasis on how AI can be used to understand how various brain-related disorders affect patients, and how AI can better assist in mental health interventions. However, I do have broad multidisciplinary research experience and am always open to collaborations outside of my main focus. See my Research page for more detailed information on projects I'm interested in working on.
