About

I am currently a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Kentucky, and a Graduate Research Assistant in the Computational Health, Imaging, and Learning Laboratory (CHILL), under the supervision of Dr. Abdullah-Al-Zubaer Imran.

My current research focus is on self-supervised learning, semi-supervised learning, and medical image segmentation. I also have extensive research experience in the area of general computer vision and machine learning applications in industry, with an emphasis on quality, reliability, and maintenance.

I received a B.S. degree in Computer Science from Morehead State University in May 2021, and an M.S. in Engineering & Technology Management from the same institution in May 2023.
